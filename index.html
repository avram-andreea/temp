The studio

Meet grip. an urban wellness studio with industrial bones and a soft, raw finish.

The grip. studio is designed as an urban sanctuary for intentional movement. Raw textures, exposed brick, soft lighting, earthy tones and architectural details come together to create a space that feels warm, grounded and quietly luxurious.

The grip.pod

Step inside the grip.pod for on-demand strength, HIIT, core, cardio and conditioning sessions. ReflectAI™ supports your form with live feedback, rep counting and personalised coaching cues, so you can train solo without feeling unguided.

30 and 45 minute sessions available.

Introducing grip.AfterDark

grip.afterdark is an after-hours reformer class designed to channel the energy of a night out—without compromising your workout.

Set to deep, rhythmic beats and low, ambient lighting, this high-intensity session blends the precision of classical Pilates with the dynamic pulse of a Friday night.

Expect sculpting sequences, elevated tempo, and an immersive atmosphere that moves with you.

Whether you're trading cocktails for core work or just want to kick off your weekend feeling powerful, grip.afterdark is your invitation to flow, burn, and glow.

Dressed in control. Lit by intention. This is your Friday night, redefined.
