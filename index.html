Classes

Three Signature Class Formats

All available across 2 difficulty levels (I & II) to suit your ability and progression.

  • grip. – Full-body strength and flexibility
  • glutes. – Lower-body focus with sculpting emphasis
  • glow. – HIIT session targeting full body, more intense, intermediate and above
  • grip.AfterDark – High-energy sessions with club-style lighting and playlists

    • Megaformer

    • grip. • glutes. • glow. • grip.AfterDark •

    A full-body, low-impact strength and endurance class performed on a resistance-based reformer machine designed to challenge control, stability, and muscular endurance. The vibe is immersive and energising: slow shakes, deep burns, dim lighting, curated playlists, and that “I’m stronger than I thought” feeling creeping in halfway through class. Benefits include improved core strength, posture, flexibility, balance, muscular tone, and joint-friendly conditioning, while also helping build long, lean muscle definition and body awareness.

    Most Popular

    Mat Pilates

    • grip. • glutes. • glow. • grip.AfterDark •

    A floor-based Pilates class focused on controlled movement, alignment, breathwork, and core activation using bodyweight and small equipment. The atmosphere feels grounding, intentional, and quietly powerful, like your nervous system finally unclenching its jaw. Regular practice can improve posture, mobility, flexibility, deep core strength, coordination, and mind-body connection, while supporting injury prevention and everyday movement quality.

    Yoga

    • vinyasa • hatha • yin •

    A movement and breath-led practice combining flowing sequences, stretches, balance work, and mindfulness to support both physical and mental wellbeing. The vibe can range from candlelit calm to powerful and sweaty depending on the style, but always leaves the room feeling softer around the edges. Yoga helps improve flexibility, mobility, balance, stress management, circulation, recovery, and mental clarity, while encouraging presence and nervous system regulation.

    HiiT

    • glow. •

    A fast-paced workout built around short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief recovery periods, designed to maximise energy output and efficiency. The atmosphere is electric and motivating, somewhere between a nightclub and a comeback montage, with high energy, strong coaching, and a team-driven push. HIIT can improve cardiovascular fitness, stamina, athletic performance, calorie burn, and overall conditioning in a shorter amount of time, while helping build resilience and confidence.
